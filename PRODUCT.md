# Product

## Register

product

## Users

WilleAI operators and creators who need to connect social accounts, prepare a post, run preflight checks, and submit a multi-platform publishing flow without leaving the workspace.

## Product Purpose

WilleAI Publish Studio is a focused publishing cockpit for the China environment. It should make the current publishing state obvious, keep account connection separate from content editing, and let users understand what blocks a publish before they submit.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, operational.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page hero layouts, decorative dashboards, overloaded single-screen cockpits, and playful visual effects that slow down repeated publishing work.

## Design Principles

- Separate workflow stages so each task has enough room.
- Keep operational status visible without turning metrics into decoration.
- Prefer familiar product UI patterns over experimental navigation.
- Make blockers concrete and close to the action they affect.
- Preserve fast keyboard and form workflows for repeated publishing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, visible focus states, minimum practical touch targets, semantic landmarks, and reduced-motion-safe transitions.
